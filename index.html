About Us

A one stop shop for everything photography, makeup, model management, event management, styling and graphics design. Founded in 2018 by two students in Lagos, Klanstyle has grown to be a trusted name in the industry with outlets in Lagos, London and Manchester.

Our Services

Make Up

We offer premium makeup services at all out studios in the UK and Nigeria as well as home service. We have attractive packages for group sessions such as bridal train. We also sell makeup kits with tailored sizes for students, working professionals or artists. Face painting services are also available at select stores.

Photography

We own the photography world. We save your memories in a way that makes them real every time you look at them. We offer out-of-location services for your photoshoot. We also offer passport photo services.

Event Coverage

Have a party? we’ll be there to record every moment. We cover all types of events and give you the best value. We offer event coverage all over the UK and Nigeria.

Graphics Design

We offer graphics design services for both individual and corporate clients. Whether it’s a one time design or retainership, we will meet all your needs in an affordable way.

Modelling & Styling

We help up and coming models break into the industry by providing a platform to showcase their talents. We offer modelling classes and offer services for makeup, styling and photography.
